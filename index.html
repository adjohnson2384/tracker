Strongman 5/3/1

Program Setup

Day 1: Explosive & Back

Main Lift: Sandbag Over Shoulder

Accessory Work

  • Chest-Supported Rows: 5 sets x 10-15 reps
  • Upper Circuit (3 Rounds):
    - DB Lateral Raises (10-15 reps)
    - Heavy Hammer Curls (10-15 reps)
    - Cable Triceps Pushdowns (10-15 reps)
  • Finisher: Landmine Anti-Rotations (8-10/side) OR Suitcase Carries

Day 2: Dinnie & Press

Main Lift: Cerberus Dinnie Pulls

Accessory Work

  • Landmine Viking Press: 4 sets x 6-10 reps
  • Upper Circuit (3 Rounds):
    - Face Pulls (10-15 reps)
    - Reverse Barbell Curls (10-15 reps)
    - Overhead Triceps Ext (10-15 reps)
  • Finisher: Ab Wheel, Pallof Press, OR Sandbag over Bar
